Rationale on UI animation & feedback: Animations are selectively applied to reflect tactile audio hardware interaction — the step sequencer traveling status light and real-time waveform visualizer maintain motion because synthesizers express dynamic energy continuously. Hover feedback on knobs, cards, and interactive controls utilizes brisk physical transitions (rotations, elevation, glow highlights) to mirror a musician's manual adjustments, ensuring fast, tactile feedback without intrusive layout shifts. Scroll reveals stagger module entries sequentially to mimic hardware racks powering on, maintaining focus on the primary hero interface. Non-audio UI elements remain static to preserve clean readability and intentional design hierarchy.
